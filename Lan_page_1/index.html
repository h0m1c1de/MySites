How We Compiled Our List
of Resume Writers

Compiling the Big List of Resume Writers
Right off the bat we faced a daunting task. There are hundreds upon hundreds of resume writing services out there. Some are sole proprietors, working out of their homes and keeping busy by word-of-mouth. Others are small to medium size firms employing a staff of writers and support personnel.

Size, Longevity, Services
and More

  • We first gathered up the most popular online resume writing services we could find. And by popular we mean those firms doing the most traffic in resumes. By the way, traffic alone is no indication of quality, as we discovered time and again.

  • We made sure their breadth of services was adequate for the typical job seeker (i.e., that they handled cover letters, related documentation, and multiple resume formats such as e-resumes and PDF resumes).

  • We made sure those popular sites had some longevity about them, that they have been in business at least long enough to have a track record that was public. And speaking of track record...

  • We made sure they were subject to an objective benchmark. Early on, we settled on Better Business Bureau scores as the objective benchmark most accepted and most widely trusted. As a bonus, it turns out that particular metric lends itself quite easily to ranking.
